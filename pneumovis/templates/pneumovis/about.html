About This Visualization


Streptococcus pneumoniae is a bacteria which causes respiratory diseases such as ear, nose, throat and lung infections. Data collected on specific strains of pneumococcus that are infecting young children in the first year of their lives. A certain vaccine, namely PCV-13, has been made in South Africa to attack specific strains of the pneumococcus however there is only a small number of strains being targeted. The purpose of this project is to implement an application which allows researchers to load new data, store data and visualize multidimensional data set. It is important to visualize data, especially large data sets, as it helps researchers to detect patterns, correlations and relationships within the data set, that may have not been easy to find when simply looking at the raw data. By the visualization of data and finding out which strains are on the rise, the PCV vaccine can be changed accordly.
